Biography

I am a PhD student at the Queensland University of Technology (QUT), researching human-centered AI and explainability for automated vehicles. My background spans mechanical engineering, autonomous vehicle control, and deep learning-based behavior analysis.

I am developing new quantitative metrics for assessing the overall health of autonomous driving agents. My thesis focuses on "A Model for Generating Human-Centric Explanations for Automated Vehicles" under the supervision of Dr. Xiaomeng Li, Prof. Andry Rakotonirainy, Prof. Ronald Schroeter, and Prof. Sebastien Glaser.
