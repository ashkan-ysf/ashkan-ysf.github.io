Biography

I am a PhD student at the Queensland University of Technology (QUT), researching human-centered AI and explainability for automated vehicles. My background spans mechanical engineering, autonomous vehicle control, and deep learning-based behavior analysis.

I am also developing new quantitative metrics for assessing the overall health of autonomous driving agents. I have spent the past 5 years as an active machine learning and explainable AI researcher across organisations such as the University of Guilan, HeyvaAI, and Cheetah AV Research Institute.
