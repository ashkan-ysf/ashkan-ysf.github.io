Biography

I am a PhD student at the Queensland University of Technology (QUT), researching human-centered AI and explainability for automated vehicles. My background spans mechanical engineering, autonomous vehicle control, and deep learning-based behavior analysis.

Currently, I focus on developing transparent algorithms for generating intelligible explanations in autonomous driving. My research aims to bridge the gap between complex AI systems and human understanding, particularly in the context of automated vehicles.
